BIOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION


Juan Pedro van Hasselt is an attorney graduated from Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú with a Master of Comparative Law degree from The George Washington University. He is admitted to practice as Counsel at the Lima Bar and as Special Legal Counsel by the District of Columbia Bar.


His intellectual property experience started at The Procter & Gamble Company where he was appointed as Senior Counsel – Trademarks for Latin America, managing +9000 marks across the American continent.


Later, he was appointed Justice of the Intellectual Property Tribunal of the Peruvian Patent and Trademark Office (INDECOPI) deciding on appeal all intellectual property matters, including decisions on a number of counterfeit issues. He issued a number of resolutions and some of them are still national jurisprudence as of today, for example: Link and Link (his name at the end of these resolutions).

We have discovered contraband rings moving illegal merchandise mostly from Peru to Ecuador, Bolivia, Chile and Argentina.


He was also Legal Director and Corporate Secretary of TIM Perú – now Claro); National Mandatary of Continental Airlines; Chief Legal Officer, Corporate Secretary and Deputy Representative to the Securities Commission (SMV) of Corporación Aceros Arequipa S.A.
